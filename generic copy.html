This project focuses on analyzing customer data to uncover patterns and insights, enabling businesses to optimize their marketing campaigns. Using SQL, the data was segmented by demographics, spending habits, and engagement levels to identify high-value customer groups. Key findings included differences in response rates across income levels, household sizes, and recency of purchases. Advanced SQL queries provided a comprehensive understanding of customer behavior, setting the stage for predictive analytics.

The project further employed machine learning techniques in Python, using Logistic Regression and Random Forest models to predict campaign responses with an accuracy of 86% and a ROC-AUC score of 0.83. Visualizations such as confusion matrices, feature importance charts, and ROC curves highlighted the models' performance and provided actionable insights. This project demonstrates expertise in data cleaning, analysis, and predictive modeling, offering valuable recommendations to enhance campaign ROI and customer engagement strategies.
